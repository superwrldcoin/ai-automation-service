# Operation: AI Automation Service

**Operator model:** You collect the money and do the human parts (publish, reply,
call). I (Claude) build the assets, write the copy, and make the strategic calls.
Everything here is legal, honest, and designed to *compound*.

---

## The thesis
Your scarce resource is not cash — it's converting AI leverage into something
people pay for. You're technical, time-rich, and already fluent in
small-business / real-estate / e-commerce operator language. So:

> Sell done-for-you **automation & AI tools** to small-business operators.
> Compete on *capability*, not price. Go where buyers already search.
> Turn every delivery into a reusable template that lowers the cost of the next sale.

Fastest path to the first dollar, needs ~$0 to start, and each job makes the next easier.

## The offer ladder
| Tier | Offer | Price (starting) | Purpose |
|------|-------|------------------|---------|
| **Entry** | "I'll automate one repetitive task" (data entry, doc generation, file cleanup, report) | $75–150 fixed | Win first reviews fast |
| **Core** | Custom automation / AI integration build (scoped, 3–7 days) | $400–1,500 fixed | The money |
| **Retainer** | "Keep it running + improvements" | $150–500/mo | Recurring, the long game |

**Beachhead niche:** real-estate investors & local SMBs (you speak their language).
**Universal wedge product:** document & data automation — every business drowns in
copy-paste work. DocForge (in `/demo`) is the proof + the reusable engine.

### Week 4 — Raise prices & pitch retainers
- [ ] With 2–3 reviews, raise entry price and push a Core build.
- [ ] Pitch a retainer to anyone whose automation needs upkeep.

## Metrics that matter (track weekly)
- Proposals/DMs sent (input you control — aim 100+/wk)
- Reply rate → call rate → close rate
- Revenue, and % from repeat/retainer (the compounding number)

## Non-negotiables
- No scraping behind logins, no spam blasts, no fake reviews, no scummy cold-texting.
- Never send client data off the machine without asking them.
- Under-promise scope, over-deliver quality. Reviews are the whole game early.
