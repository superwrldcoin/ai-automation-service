# Cold outreach playbook
Distribution is the bottleneck for a no-audience start. This is the engine.
**The rule: volume + relevance. 100+ touches/week. Personalize the first line.**

---

## Where to find targets (free, no scraping-behind-logins)
- **Upwork/Fiverr buyer requests** — people literally posting they need automation. Warmest.
- **Reddit**: r/smallbusiness, r/realestateinvesting, r/Entrepreneur, r/wholesaling —
  search "manual", "spreadsheet", "so tedious", "wish I could automate". Reply helpfully,
  DM only if they're open to it. Never spam threads.
- **Facebook groups** for local investors / SMB owners — answer questions, become known.
- **Local business directories / Google Maps** — small firms with clearly manual ops
  (property management, small agencies, clinics). Email their public address.
- **Indie Hackers / X** — founders complaining about busywork.

## The approach that works
Lead with *their pain and a specific fix*, not "I do automation." Offer a tiny free
thing (a mini audit or a sample) to start a conversation. You're time-rich — use it
to be genuinely helpful first. That's your unfair advantage over paid-ad competitors.

---

## Template A — Upwork/Fiverr proposal (respond to a posted job)
> Hi [Name] — I can build exactly this. The [specific task they described] is a
> classic mail-merge/data-automation job; I've built the same pattern before and can
> turn it around in [X days]. It'll run on your machine, no subscriptions, and you'll
> own the code. Quick question so I scope it right: [one sharp question about their data].
> Happy to show a working sample first so you know it's real. — [You]

## Template B — Cold email to a small business
> Subject: the [specific task] your team does by hand
>
> Hi [Name], I noticed [specific observation — e.g., "you send custom quotes to each
> client"]. If that's still manual, I build small tools that generate them from a
> spreadsheet in one click — the kind of thing that saves a few hours a week.
> I'll build a working sample with your format free, no strings, so you can see it
> before deciding anything. Worth a 10-minute look? — [You]

## Template C — Warm DM (after helping in a thread)
> Hey, glad that helped. This is literally what I do — I build little automations for
> [their task]. If you ever want it done-for-you instead of DIY, I'll put together a
> quick sample with your data at no cost. No pressure either way.

---

## Handling the first reply
1. **Book a 15-min call** (or async Q&A). Goal: understand the task exactly.
2. **Offer a free/cheap sample** on their real data — proof crushes objections.
3. **Quote a fixed price** (never hourly early — you're fast, hourly punishes speed).
4. **Deliver + walkthrough video + ask for a review the same day.**

## Objection → response
- "Too expensive" → "Here's the entry version at $[X] to prove it, then scale up."
- "I'll just use ChatGPT" → "Great for one-offs; I build the repeatable tool so you
  never redo it. Let me show you the difference on your data."
- "How do I know it works?" → "I'll build a working sample first, free."
