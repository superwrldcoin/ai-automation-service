# Delivery playbook — getting the tool into a non-technical client's hands

The build is the easy part. **Delivery is where automation businesses win or lose.** A
contractor who can't open the tool will never pay you again. This is the exact system to
hand it over so a 60-year-old plumber who "isn't good with computers" uses it every day.

Core principle: **they should never type a web address, install anything, or read a manual.**
They tap an icon. That's it.

---

## The big picture (how distribution actually works)
- The **public site on GitHub Pages is your SALES demo** — one shared version for prospects.
- Each **paying client gets their OWN private copy** that you host for them (free), with their
  pricebook, prices, logo, and business name baked in. That copy is "the deliverable."
- You deliver it as: **an icon on their phone + a printed cheat sheet + a 3-minute video.**

---

## STEP 1 — Build their private copy (you + me)
1. Get from the client: their **logo**, **business name**, **service list + prices**, **shop ZIP**,
   and **tax rate**. (Ask for these when they say yes — "send me these 5 things and I'll build it.")
2. Hand them to me (Claude). I copy the tool, drop in their pricebook and branding, and set
   the defaults so it opens ready-to-use for their trade.

## STEP 2 — Host it for free (you)
Pick ONE. All are free and give you a clickable link:

**Option A — Netlify Drop (easiest, no account needed to start)**
1. Go to **app.netlify.com/drop**.
2. Drag the client's folder onto the page.
3. It gives you a link like `their-business.netlify.app`. Done. (Free account lets you rename it.)

**Option B — A folder on your existing GitHub Pages site**
1. Put their copy in `docs/clients/their-name/`.
2. Push. Their link is `superwrldcoin.github.io/ai-automation-service/clients/their-name/`.
   (I can do this part for you.)

**Option C — Their own domain (upsell, looks most pro)**
- Buy a cheap domain (~$10/yr) or a subdomain like `quotes.theirbusiness.com` and point it
  at the hosting. Charge this back to them as part of the setup.

> Whichever you pick, the tool is already a **PWA** — it installs like an app and works offline.

## STEP 3 — Put the icon on their phone (do this WITH them, in person or on a screen-share)
This is the single most important step. **Do it for them the first time.**

**On an iPhone (Safari):**
1. Open their link in **Safari** (must be Safari, not Chrome).
2. Tap the **Share** button (the square with an arrow).
3. Scroll down, tap **"Add to Home Screen."**
4. Tap **"Add."** Now there's an app icon on their phone.

**On an Android phone (Chrome):**
1. Open their link in **Chrome**.
2. Tap the **three dots** (top right).
3. Tap **"Add to Home screen"** (or **"Install app"**).
4. Tap **"Add" / "Install."**

**On a computer (Chrome/Edge):**
- Open the link, click the **install icon** in the address bar (a little screen/⊕), click Install.
- Or just add a browser bookmark and pin it.

Now they open it like any app. **They will never see a web address again.**

## STEP 4 — Give them the cheat sheet + video
1. **Cheat sheet:** open `how-to.html`, fill in the 3 blanks (their link, your name/phone),
   print it, and hand them a physical copy. Also save it as a PDF and text it to them.
2. **Video:** record a 2–3 minute screen recording (free tools: Loom, or the built-in
   phone screen recorder) doing one real quote start to finish. Text them the link.
   People re-watch this instead of calling you.

## STEP 5 — The first-week check-in (this earns the retainer)
- Day 2: text — *"Did you get a chance to make a quote with it? Any snags?"*
- Day 7: quick call — watch them do one, fix anything confusing.
- This is where they decide you're worth keeping on a monthly retainer. Do not skip it.

---

## How to EXPLAIN it to the client (say it this simply — 8th-grade level)
Use these exact kinds of words. No tech talk.

- **What it is:** *"It's a button on your phone. You tap it, fill in a few boxes, and it makes
  a clean, professional quote in about a minute — instead of you doing it by hand at night."*
- **Do I have to install anything?** *"Nope. Nothing to download. I set it up for you. You just tap the icon."*
- **What if I have no signal?** *"It still works. You can make a quote in a basement or out in the sticks."*
- **Is my customer info safe?** *"Yes. Everything stays on your own phone. It's not posted online or shared."*
- **What if I mess it up?** *"You can't break it. And if you get stuck, you call me — that's what I'm here for."*
- **The follow-up part:** *"It also keeps a list of everyone you gave a price to and tells you who
  to call back, so you stop losing jobs you forgot about. That one thing usually pays for the whole tool."*
