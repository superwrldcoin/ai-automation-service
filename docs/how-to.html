2. Make a quote (about 1 minute)

1

Type the customer's name and phone number in the top boxes.

2

Type their address and tap "Look up address." The tool adds the drive-time charge for you automatically.

3

Check the boxes next to the work you're doing. You can change any price right there if you need to.

4

Tap the big "Generate quote" button. That's it — your finished quote pops up on the screen.

💡 In a hurry? Type what the customer needs in plain words in the top box (like "AC tune-up and a new thermostat") and tap Analyze. The tool fills in the quote for you.

3. Send it to the customer

1

Tap "Print / Save as PDF."

2

Choose "Save as PDF." This makes a neat file.

3

Text or email that file to your customer, just like sending a photo.

💡 To turn a quote into a bill after the job, tap the "Invoice" button at the top. Everything stays the same — it just becomes a bill.

4. Never lose a job again (the Follow-up list)

The second button is your Follow-up list. Every time you give someone a price, add it here. The list turns red when it's time to call them back.

1

Type the customer, the job, and the price. Tap "Add."

2

Each day, open it and call anyone in the red or yellow rows.

3

When you win the job, tap "Won." Done.

💡 The number at the top shows the dollars waiting on a callback. That's money you'd usually forget about. This is your daily to-do list.

Your information is private. Everything you type stays on your own phone or computer. It is not sent to the internet or shared with anyone.
